Advanced Concepts Team

About:

Space Hopper is a serious game developed and maintained by the Advanced Concepts Team of the European Space Agency as an open source project. You are welcome to help us develop the game or fork it. In any case, should you use the code or get inspired by our ideas, please consider quoting the following works:
  • Piotrowski, W., Martens, M., Izzo, D., and Hennes, D., Space Hopper: a Serious Game Crowdsourcing the Design of Interplanetary Trajectories, Acta Futura, 9, pp.93-100, 2014. (pdf here)
  • Stefan Willi et al., Space Hopper: results of the first crowdsourcing experiment, to appear in 2015
